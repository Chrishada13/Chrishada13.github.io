Lies of P

Are you a human or puppet?

Story

Lies of P is set in the late 19th Century to the early 20th Century of the Belle Ã‰poque Era in Europe within a city called Krat. The city of Krat was once a beautiful place but has now become lost and turned into a living hell that is filled with unspeakable horrors due to a deadly plague. Krat is featured as a grim city where multiple automatons now roam almost every part of the center, from the streets, rooftops, alleyways, and within buildings. As you start your adventure in Lies of P, you play as the puppet mechanoid Pinocchio who awakens in an abandoned train station not knowing where he has come from and what his purpose is. As we all know the famous fable of Pinocchio, Mr. Geppetto carved a wooden puppet named Pinocchio and was visited by the Blue Fairy who granted Mr. Geppetto's wish by transforming the puppet into a living being, that being said, Pinocchio sets forth into reaching his goal of becoming a real boy.

Announcement

Lies of P, a new action Souls-like inspired game was officially announced with a trailer back in May 2021. It highlighted a cinematic trailer featuring Mr. Geppetto, the creator of the famous puppet, Pinocchio, as well as a sneak peek of the new style of the now puppet mechanoid, Pinocchio. Lies of P Game Developer and Publisher NEOWIZ Games, alongside Game Developer Round8 Studio, highlighted a unique feature called the "Lie" System where what players do and choose to say as Pinocchio will dynamically affect various points of gameplay.
